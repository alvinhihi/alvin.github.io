Easy chicken curry

Ingredient:

  • 1 smalll onion, garlic and ginger, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons curry pwder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • Half cup coconut milk

Steps:

    Steps1 - Ingredients preparation
  1. Seal the chicken in a pan with a little oil
  2. Add a chopped onion, garlic and ginger
    1. Cook for 5 minuted to soften
    2. Add in curry powder and cinnamo
  3. Add in the tomato paste and coconut milk
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes - until the chicken is cooked through
